 The Heber Fielding Burton Family Organization serves the descendents of Heber Fielding Burton and Mary Delecta Ballantyne as well as anyone interested in the family, its genealogy and history. One of the primary functions of the organization is to sponsor the family reunions which take place once every three years. The organization is also charged with coordinating genealogical research for the family.
